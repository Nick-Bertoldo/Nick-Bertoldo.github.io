I'm currently a PhD fellow at Polimi. I work in the field of sustainability, applying the Life Cycle Assessment methodology (LCA).

Research:

My research is focused on public procurement and how to make the procurement process more sustainable. The primary goal is to Support, Simplify and Promote Sustainable Public Procurement practices by Public Authorities. This will be achieved through the development of a Life Cycle thinking-based decision-support system designed as a multi-criteria approach, allowing the comparisons of the bids based on their compliance with Green Public Procurement requirements, environmental impact assessments, circularity, and economic cost considerations.
